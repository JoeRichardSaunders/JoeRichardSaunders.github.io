About

Joe Richard Saunders began doing stand-up comedy in Washington DC after a decade spent in music production and performance (both nationally and internationally). His stand-up routine features jokes and stories that intersect with topics such as growing up racially ambiguous, navigating awkward interpersonal encounters, culture, and technology. Joe is a regular performer in Maryland, Washington DC, and Virginia, and has also taken his comedy to Boston, MA, and Austin, TX. He has performed on the main stages of notable venues such as The 9:30 Club in Washington DC, The DCImprov, Magooby's Joke House in Maryland, and The Creek and The Cave in Texas.

Genres, Interests, and Subject Matter: Anti-humor, cringe comedy, culture, everyday life, improvisational comedy, music, observational comedy, race relations, racism, satire, self-deprecation, sketch comedy, stand up comedy, surreal comedy
