About

Joe Richard Saunders is a Baltimore-based comedian with a background in music production. His stand-up routine features jokes and stories that intersect with topics such as growing up racially ambiguous, navigating awkward interpersonal encounters, culture, and technology. Joe is a regular performer in Maryland, Washington DC, Pennsylvania, and Virginia, and he is enthusiastic about using comedy proceeds to support cancer research, patient access, policy, and advocacy.

Genres, Interests, and Subject Matter: Anti-humor, cringe comedy, culture, everyday life, improvisational comedy, music, observational comedy, race relations, racism, satire, self-deprecation, sketch comedy, stand up comedy, surreal comedy

Harm City Comedy

I produce Harm City Comedy with 3 other comedian friends. We aim to highlight up-and-coming talent in Baltimore city. So far, our shows average about 50 attendees. If you are interested in performing on a Harm City Comedy show, please reach out to our social media team.
