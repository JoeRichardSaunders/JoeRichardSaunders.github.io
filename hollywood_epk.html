THE PITCH

I am a working comedian and 2x life-threatening cancer survivor as a young adult who produces indie comedy shows in Baltimore and Washington DC. I consult for writers' rooms, non-profits, healthcare organizations, and universities that need dark humor, technical accuracy, and a reality check on cancer "resilience." As a standup comedian, I have performed on shows with Mary Beth Barone, Chris Kattan, Luke Severeid, Dan St. Germain, Felicia Folkes, and more. I can be seen regularly performing in Baltimore, Washington DC, Viriginia, and Pennslyvania.

FILM & TV SERVICES

  • Script Accuracy Pass: Reviewing scripts for authentic depiction of academia, hospitals, oncology wards, Baltimore and Washington DC life, shipyard operations, and engineering factories.
  • Room Punch-Up: Adding "Smartest Idiot" dialogue and "Middle Class" perspective to character arcs.
  • Character Backstory: Developing realistic "Engineering/PhD burnout" or "Young Adult Survivor" archetypes that avoid Hollywood tropes.

HIGHER ED & SPEAKING

  • Talk: "No One Cares, Man" – An invited keynote on the realities of surviving cancer as a young adult. Ulman Foundation: Screw Cancer, Brew Hope event 2025
  • Workshop: Humor in Healthcare – For medical students and nursing programs on bedside manner and the young adult cancer patient reality.
  • Video Series: HOW TO GET INTO STANDUP COMEDY: No One Cares, Man – A comedian's outlook applied to a modern tutorial for newcomers.
