Joe Richard Saunders is a comedian, writer, and two-time cancer survivor whose superpower is indifference to the miraculous. After beating Stage 4 Lymphoma and a brain tumor, he realized the world doesn't pause for your worst day—it just gives you a parking ticket outside the hospital. Operating under the moniker "No One Cares, Man," Joe brings his dark, bureaucratic survival humor to stages across the country, having performed alongside Mary Beth Barone, Chris Kattan, and Luke Severeid.
